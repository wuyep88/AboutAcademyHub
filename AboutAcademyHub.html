'The Academy Hub' was an intiative of Engineer O.O.Tinuoye along side his partner, Engineer A.Adebayo in 2019.
The hub was established with the hope of recruiting young and vibrant youths and equiping them with the necessary skills needed to leverage on the emerging technologies.
It also aimed at becoming a solution cetre, where different IT problems would be solved.
  • To create a condusive learning environment
  • To create a solution providing Centre
  • To create a well equiped cetre for acquiring IT related skills
  • To provide internet access to the community
  • To provide training to willing and serious persons at a subsudised rate
  • To train as many persons as possible in different fields of IT
  • To provide an easy access to IT equipments to as many persons as possible
